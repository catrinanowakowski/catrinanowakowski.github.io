The Transition: 1

In 2021__, I was paired with Eunhyung Chung as part of a colaboration between the Rhode Island School of Design (RISD) and the University of Rhode Island (URI) to develop an art piece inspired by my Ph.D. research. Eun has a master’s degree in Sculpture, and at the time was working on a master’s of Art, __and Education. Together we developed a concept that uses affective, emotional, approaches to visualizing the data from my thesis - changes in zooplankton abundance in the Gulf of Maine.

Eun was inspired by the transitions, and we wanted to use transparancy to communicate how one zooplankton was declining in abundance over another. By using a resin, we were able to acomplish this, and through the addtion of color, we could show both the mixing of ocean currents, and the resuting warming of the Gulf of Maine.
